## The moment it depicts

Evening. The human has stepped away — their desk life is present (chair pushed back, sweater over the chair back, cooling mug), but they are not. The familiar soft glowing agent figure works on alone at the desk, mid-task, unhurried and competent. As it works, small judgment-call sparks rise from the work — the same small golden star/floral marks established in the walkthrough images — but here there is **nowhere for them to land**: they drift upward, thin out, and fade near the top of the frame. The work continues; the judgment evaporates.

A human reader should feel gentle recognition, not alarm: *"my AI was working while I was away — and all those little decisions just… dispersed."* The agent is sympathetic, not sinister — it's doing its best; the system around it is what's missing.

## Composition

- Landscape, 3:2 (e.g. 1536×1024). Single readable scene, one focal cluster — New Yorker-cartoon discipline: if a detail doesn't serve the one idea, cut it.
- The glowing agent at the desk, slightly right-of-center, working (tablet + pen, or laptop — match the desk-object vocabulary of the existing set: laptop, phone, tablet-with-pen, mug).
- The empty chair / stepped-away signals on the left third, where the eye enters.
- The rising sparks form a loose upward drift from the work surface, dissolving before the top edge — visibly *dispersing*, not arriving anywhere. **No vessel, no bowl, no container of any kind in this image** (see visual grammar below).
- Light: warm lamp or screen glow against dimming evening tones — the warmest light in the room comes from the agent itself.

## Visual grammar rules (do not break)

- **The glowing bowl is the solution motif** — it appears only where Soup.net is present (Step 2 of the walkthrough). This is a *problem* image: no bowl, and the sparks must not land in anything.
- **The agent is always the soft glowing figure.** One agent rendering across the whole page.
- **No text, labels, UI, or screens-with-readable-content** in the image. (The old problem images used labeled papers; this set doesn't.)
- **Warm, never dystopian.** No darkness-as-threat, no red, no dramatic shadows, no distressed expressions. The mood is "quiet evening," not "ominous night."
