## The one idea

**The recipe book is the connector now, not you.** The same human from the judgment-hub image, now relaxed and delighted, among their devices — phone, laptop, tablet (different tools, different vendors). Their agents are at **full warm glow**. Above/behind everything, ONE shared constellation of small recipe cards — organized into a few clear neighborhoods per the established Step-3 conventions — and the golden spark-trails now flow **between each device's work and the constellation**, connected, instead of dispersing separately. The human contributes a single golden judgment mark once — up to the constellation — and it reaches every agent from there.

One-glance read: *"all my tools, one accumulated judgment — and I only have to say it once."*

## The scene

- The same human (brown hair, soft sweater — match `illustration-judgement-hub.png` exactly), posture open and at ease; one hand releasing a single golden mark upward toward the constellation. Expression: quiet delight, not labor.
- Two or three devices arranged naturally (laptop, tablet-with-pen, phone), each attended by an agent figure at **full radiance** (the canonical soft glowing humanoid at its warmest — these agents carry the user's taste and judgment now).
- The constellation: one connected field of small recipe-card marks with dotted constellation lines, 3–5 loose neighborhoods (reuse the visual language of `illustration-shared-book.png`'s central cloud and `illustration-context-returning.png`'s clusters). It spans the composition so every device visibly connects to it.
- Spark-trails: golden marks flowing up from each agent's work into the constellation AND back down to the others — circulation, not dispersal. The contrast with the hub image's three separate fading plumes is the argument.
- Light: warm, time-neutral. No bowl (Step 2 owns the bowl; the constellation owns this image).
